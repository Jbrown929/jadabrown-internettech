About

As an avid music lover, Jada Brown knew she wanted to make being in music her career as a child. Being around her father, a New Orleans' R&B artist who went by the name of Ruda Real, and her grandfather Lewis Parker, a bassist in a local band, she grew her passion for music from a young age. At the age of thirteen, Jada began playing the trombone, baritone, and Euphonium in her high school band as well as taking classes in electronic music. In 2019, at the age of seventeen, she began her college career majoring in Music Industry Studies: Business track. That year she was also able to earn her first internship at Jazz Crawl Productions as an artist management intern. There she began to learn the ins and outs of the industry by aiding local New Orleans artists in their careers, doing work in web copywriting, social media campaigns, and proposals; she got experience in creative work by engaging in photo editing, video editing, and filming. From there, she joined the international team of DearBrand Productions, a licensing company that specializes in Asian music. There she was able to begin gaining experience in synch licensing and email campaigns. Currently, She is working under the New Orleans Saints football team as an entertainment staff member learning and gaining experience in the world of live entertainment. She lives in New Orleans and is looking forward to finishing her college career in 2023.
